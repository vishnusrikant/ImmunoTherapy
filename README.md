# ImmunoTherapy

Topic: Using AI to Predict Side Effects of ImmunoTherapy to Treat Cancer In Patients

Side Effects of a specific cancer Immunotherapy are based on what conditions
* Type of Cancer
* Age
* Gender
* Previous Autoimmune Conditions
* Family History of Autoimmunity
* BMI
* Health Markers (large category --> Inflamatory markers
                                 --> specify by datasets)
* Prior Treatments or Simultaneous Treatments

Based on AI modelling - given the patients bio data, we can predict the Side Effects of Immunotherapy being : Mild, Medium or Severe
